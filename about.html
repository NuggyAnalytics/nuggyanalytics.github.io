Our Mission

We're on a mission to transform the cannabis industry's notoriously thin margins into sustainable, profitable businesses. Nuggy is your personal pricing buddy, designed specifically for dispensaries ready to embrace data-driven growth.

Why Cannabis Pricing Matters

The cannabis industry operates on some of the thinnest margins in retail. Between compliance costs, taxes, and fierce competition, dispensaries struggle to maintain profitability.

Traditional pricing strategies don't work in cannabis. Product diversity, regulatory constraints, and rapid market changes require a smarter approach.

That's where Nuggy comes in. We've built AI specifically for cannabis retail, understanding the unique challenges you face every day.
