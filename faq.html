Answers to common questions about Nuggy.

General Questions

What is Nuggy?

Nuggy is an AI-powered pricing optimization platform designed specifically for cannabis dispensaries. We use advanced algorithms to analyze your inventory, costs, and market conditions to recommend optimal pricing strategies that maximize your profits while staying competitive.

How does Nuggy work?

Our platform integrates with your dispensary's existing systems to analyze real-time data including inventory levels, product costs, competitor pricing, and market demand. Our AI then generates dynamic pricing recommendations for all your products - from flower and edibles to concentrates and accessories.

What makes Nuggy different from other pricing tools?

Unlike generic pricing software, Nuggy is built specifically for the cannabis industry. Our algorithms understand the unique characteristics of different product categories, seasonal variations, and the specific regulatory environment of cannabis retail. We provide real-time cost analysis combined with dynamic pricing recommendations.

Pilot Program

What is the pilot program?

Our pilot program offers early access to Nuggy's full platform with exclusive benefits for participating dispensaries. We're currently accepting only 10 dispensaries to ensure personalized support and gather valuable feedback to enhance our platform.

What are the benefits of joining the pilot program?

Pilot partners receive:

  • Exclusive pricing locked in for future use
  • Priority customer support
  • Direct input on feature development
  • Early access to new features
  • Special recognition as a founding partner

How many spots are available in the pilot program?

We have limited the pilot program to 10 dispensaries to ensure quality support and implementation. Spots are filling quickly.

How long does the pilot program last?

The pilot program duration varies but typically runs for 3-6 months, allowing enough time to see meaningful results and optimize the platform for your specific needs.

Data & Security

Is my dispensary data secure?

Yes. We use bank-level encryption and security protocols to protect all your data. We're fully compliant with cannabis industry regulations and never share your data with competitors.

Who has access to my pricing data?

Only authorized users from your dispensary can access your data. We never share individual dispensary data with other clients or third parties.

Do you sell or share data with third parties?

No. Your data remains confidential and is used solely to provide pricing recommendations for your dispensary.
